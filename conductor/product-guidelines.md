# Product Guidelines: Munchy

## Design Aesthetic
Munchy's interface combines **Clean and Modern** principles with a **Friendly and Warm** feel. This ensures a professional, high-quality experience that remains inviting and accessible. Use whitespace effectively to avoid clutter, while incorporating warm accents to make the tracking process feel supportive rather than clinical.

## Voice and Tone
- **Casual and Friendly:** The app should communicate like a supportive companion. Avoid overly technical jargon when explaining nutritional data.
- **Encouraging:** Celebrate progress and provide positive reinforcement for consistent logging habits.

## UX Principles
- **Efficiency-Focused:** Minimize the steps required for core tasks, especially meal logging. Provide smart suggestions and quick-add options.
- **Consistent and Predictable:** Maintain a uniform layout and interaction pattern across all screens (e.g., standard dialog behaviors, consistent button placement) to build user familiarity and trust.

## Mobile UX Strategy
- **Mobile-First Accessibility:** Given the "on-the-go" nature of meal logging, ensure all interactive elements are touch-friendly and the interface remains fully functional on small screens.
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Clearly surface the most important metrics (e.g., remaining calories) at the top of the mobile dashboard for immediate visibility.
