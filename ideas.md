# Munchy Feature Brainstorming: Elevating the Experience for All Users

## 1. Frictionless Logging (Maximum Convenience)

- **Advanced Barcode Scanning:** A fast, reliable barcode scanner that instantly pulls nutritional data from an extensive database (like Open Food Facts or USDA).
- **"Quick Add" & Recent Foods:** A highly accessible list of the user's most frequently logged items and recent meals, allowing for 1-tap logging.
- **Copy Yesterday's Meal:** An option to instantly copy an entire meal (e.g., Breakfast) from a previous day to the current day.
- **Custom Recipe Builder:** Allow users to manually combine multiple ingredients into a single "Recipe" (e.g., "Mom's Lasagna") and log portions of it effortlessly in the future.
- **Offline Logging Support:** Allow users to log food even when they don't have an internet connection. The app queues the data and syncs with the database once the connection is restored.

## 2. Advanced Insights & Personalization (For Power Users & Fitness Enthusiasts)

- **Day-Specific Macro Goals (Carb Cycling):** Allow users to set different calorie and macro targets for different days of the week (e.g., higher carbs and calories on heavy training days, lower on rest days).
- **Net Carb Tracking:** A toggle for Keto or low-carb users to automatically subtract dietary fiber (and sugar alcohols) from total carbohydrates to track "Net Carbs."
- **Weight Trend & Projection Graphs:** Interactive charts that plot the user's weight over time against their caloric intake, providing an estimated date for reaching their goal weight based on their current average deficit or surplus.
- **Wearable & Health App Integration:** Sync with Apple Health, Google Fit, Garmin, etc., to automatically import daily steps and active calories burned, dynamically adjusting the user's remaining daily calorie allowance.
- **Granular Data Export:** Allow power users to export their entire nutrition and weight history as a CSV or JSON file for analysis in Excel, Notion, or custom dashboards.
- **Custom Micronutrient Goals:** Allow users to set specific daily targets for individual micronutrients (e.g., 3000mg Potassium, 150g Protein, 30g Fiber) and track them on the dashboard.

## 3. Planning & Organization

- **Weekly Meal Planner:** A calendar interface where users can plan their meals days or weeks in advance, ensuring they hit their macro targets before the day even begins.
- **Automated Grocery List Generator:** Based on the meals planned for the upcoming week in the Meal Planner, the app automatically generates a consolidated grocery list.
- **Coach/Trainer Sharing Portal:** Allow users to generate a secure, read-only link to their food logs and weight progress, making it easy to share data with a personal trainer, registered dietitian, or accountability partner.

## 4. Gamification & Retention

- **Logging Consistency Streaks:** Track how many consecutive days the user has logged their food to build habit formation.
- **Water & Hydration Tracking:** A simple, quick-tap interface for tracking daily water intake alongside food.
- **Weekly "Wrap-Up" Reports:** A summary dashboard at the end of each week showing average daily calories, macro adherence percentage, and highlighting the user's most nutrient-dense meals.
